A collection of iPython Notebooks relating to PhenoCam processing. The PhenoCam Project website is hosted at:

phenocam.sr.unh.edu

The PhenoCam project is an NSF funded collaboration between Harvard University, Boston University, Washington University (St. Louis) and the University of New Hampshire.
